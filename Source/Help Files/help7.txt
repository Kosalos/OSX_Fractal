7 Save/Load Dialog

Press 'D' to select sorting entries by Date
Press 'D' again to toggle between Ascending and Descending dates

Press 'K' to sort entries by fractal kind

Press 'S' to add the current fractal settings to the list

Click on entry for immediate Load

Click on entry 'Overwrite' button to replace that dataset
      with the current fractal data

Click on entry 'Delete' button to remove it from the list

Click/Drag mouse over list to multi-select entries for deletion
